Chance The Rapper is 25 years old and was born on April 16,1993. His birthplace is Chicago, IL. He started rapping at a young age but gained recognition following the release of his second mixtape, Acid Rap. May 2016, Bennett (The music company) released his third mixtape Coloring Book to critical acclaim. It earned him three Grammy Awards, including Best Rap Album. Winning this award, it then became the first streaming-only album to win a Grammy Award. Recently he dropped his new single " I Might Need Security, and I absolutely love it.
